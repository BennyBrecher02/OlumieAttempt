In fast-moving situations, the goal isn’t “more diligence.” The goal is **the right diligence**.

## What we prioritize
- clarity on constraints and approvals
- capital structure and liquidity needs
- a short list of operational levers that matter early

When those are clear, speed becomes a byproduct of alignment.
